FAQ

Questions we get asked

Do I need to install anything?
No. Cherry Pharmacy runs entirely in your browser. Any device, any OS. Open it and start billing. No downloads, no IT support, no updates to manage.
What happens if my internet goes down?
Nothing stops. Billing, stock updates, and shift management all continue offline. When your connection returns, everything syncs automatically in the background.
Does it support my country's tax system?
Yes. Whether it's GST (India), VAT (Zimbabwe, UK, EU, and most of the world), or another structure, tax is calculated per line item on every bill. Month-end CSV exports are formatted for direct submission to your accountant.
Can I manage multiple branches?
Pro and Enterprise plans support multi-branch management with a consolidated dashboard, inter-branch transfers, and separate staff access per branch.
How long does setup take?
Most pharmacies are fully set up — inventory loaded, staff accounts created, first bill printed — within the same day. No training sessions needed.
Can I switch plans later?
Yes. Upgrade or downgrade any time. Your data stays intact. Changes take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.
